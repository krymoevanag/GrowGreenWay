What is Sustainable Farming?

Sustainable farming is an eco-friendly approach to agriculture that focuses on conserving natural resources, enhancing soil health, and reducing environmental impact. By using organic techniques, water-efficient methods, and biodiversity-friendly practices, farmers can produce healthier crops while protecting the planet.
